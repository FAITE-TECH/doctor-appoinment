Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?

Sign up for an account, browse available doctors, and select your preferred appointment time.

Can I cancel my appointment?

Yes, you can cancel appointments up to 24 hours before the scheduled time through your account dashboard.

What if I need to reschedule?

You can reschedule appointments by logging into your account and selecting a new available time slot.
